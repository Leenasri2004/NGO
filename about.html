Our Values

What Guides Every Step We Take

Nathi Foundation is built on simple but strong values that shape how we plan, spend and serve.

Compassion

We see each child, woman and family as valuable, and respond with kindness and respect in every situation.

Partnership

We work together with local leaders, schools, churches and volunteers instead of working alone.

Integrity

We handle funds carefully, keep promises and communicate honestly with our communities and supporters.

Where We Work

Rooted In Our Local Communities

Our team lives and works close to the families we serve in and around Tiruppathur and neighbouring villages.

Tiruppathur Town

After‑school centres, women’s groups and youth clubs run in partnership with local organisations.

Nearby Villages

Regular health camps, home visits and relief support in surrounding rural communities.

Schools & Churches

Awareness sessions and special programmes hosted through trusted local partners and institutions.

Who We Are

Bringing hope to Tamil Nadu communities

Nathi Foundation is a registered non‑profit organisation based in Tiruppathur, Tamil Nadu. We work with children, women and families from under‑resourced backgrounds to ensure access to education, healthcare and sustainable livelihoods.

Since [Year Founded], our volunteers and partners have been running community programmes, awareness campaigns and long‑term projects in both urban and rural areas. We believe in transparency, accountability and community‑driven development in everything we do.

Vanathi Sivakumar
Founder & Director

Our Approach

How We Work With Communities

From village‑level meetings to school‑based programmes, we co‑create solutions with community members so that projects are relevant, owned locally and sustainable in the long run.

View Our Projects
1. Understand Needs

Baseline surveys, home visits and group discussions help us map local challenges in education, health and livelihoods.

2. Co‑design Programmes

We plan initiatives with panchayat leaders, teachers, health workers and volunteers.

3. Implement & Mentor

Regular sessions, camps and trainings are conducted with continuous mentoring support.

4. Measure Impact

We track learning outcomes, health indicators and income improvements to refine programmes.
