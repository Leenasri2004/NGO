How We Work

How A Program Becomes Real Help

Every program at Nathi Foundation follows a simple, careful journey – from listening to a need, to planning, action and long‑term follow‑up.

01
Listen & Learn

We start by meeting local leaders, parents and children to understand their real challenges.

02
Design The Program

Our team plans simple, practical activities that fit the community’s needs and strengths.

03
Work Alongside

Staff, volunteers and partners run the program together and with the regular visits to their field .

04
Measure & Grow

We track progress, share updates with supporters and improve the program every year.

What We Do

Programs That Bring Hope And Lasting Change

Each program is designed to address a specific need in our community – from education and health to livelihoods and emergency relief.

Child Education Support

After-school tuition, mentoring and basic learning materials for children from low-income families in [Area/Village].

Women Empowerment

Skill-building, self-help groups and counselling support that help women gain confidence and income.

Health & Nutrition

Community health camps, anaemia awareness and nutrition support for vulnerable children and elders.

Livelihood & Skills

Vocational training in tailoring, computer basics and small-business guidance for youth and women.

Community Development

Awareness sessions on child protection, environment, hygiene and rights conducted with local groups.

Emergency Relief

Rapid response with food kits, essentials and follow-up care during floods, pandemics or other crises.
