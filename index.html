Our Mission

A caring neighbour for every vulnerable family in Tiruppathur and beyond.

We work in education, health and women’s empowerment so that children stay in school, families stay healthy and women build stable livelihoods.

We believe that long-term change happens when local communities are equipped, women are empowered, and children receive the care and opportunities they deserve.

Our Programs

What We Do

Our core programs focus on education, health and livelihoods so that families can move from crisis to stability.

Education Support

After‑school learning centers, tuition support and school kits so that children stay in school and excel.

Health & Nutrition

Village‑level health camps, check‑ups and nutrition support for children and pregnant women.

Women Empowerment

Skill training, self‑help groups and livelihood support that help women earn and save with dignity.
