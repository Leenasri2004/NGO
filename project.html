Project Pillars

Four Ways Our Projects Bring Change

Every Nathi Foundation project fits into one of these simple pillars so that our work stays focused and effective.

Learning & Child Care

After‑school centers, school kits and mentoring to keep children learning and safe.

Health & Nutrition

Health camps, home visits and basic nutrition support for families in crisis.

Women’s Livelihoods

Tailoring, small business skills and tools so women can earn with dignity.

Youth & Community

Youth clubs, awareness drives and neighbourhood‑level service projects.

Our Journey

Growing Impact Year By Year

A few milestones that show how Nathi Foundation has been steadily walking with families in need.

2016 – First Learning Center

Tiruppathur

We started our very first after‑school learning center with 20 children from nearby communities.

2019 – Health Camps Expand

Rural Villages

Regular health camps began in multiple villages, serving children, women and elderly people.

2022 – Women Skill Training

Women Empowerment

Dozens of women completed tailoring and craft courses and started earning stable side income.

2025 – Youth Leadership Clubs

Youth Development

Young people began leading local service projects, tutoring children and organizing awareness drives.

One Child

A student from our learning center in Tiruppathur now attends school regularly because of homework support and encouragement from our mentors.

One Family

One Village

Project Impact

What These Projects Are Achieving

Behind every project are real numbers – children in class, women trained and families receiving care.

Key Outcomes So Far
Children attending learning centers 80%
Women completing skill training 65%
Families receiving health & nutrition support 72%
Why These Numbers Matter

Each percentage represents children who are staying in school, women who are now earning an income, and families who are healthier and more secure.

  • Learning centers keep children safe and focused after school hours.
  • Skill programs open doors to small businesses and long‑term stability for women.
  • Health and nutrition support reduces crisis situations for vulnerable families.
  • Your partnership helps us move these numbers closer to 100% every year.
