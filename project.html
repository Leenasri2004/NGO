Our Projects

Impact Stories From The Field

Here are some of the key projects through which we serve children, women and families in our communities.

After‑School Learning Centers
Mentoring and nutrition support for children from low‑income families in Tiruppathur.
Health Camps & Check‑ups
Regular health camps in rural communities with free check‑ups and basic medicines for children.
Women Skill Training
Vocational training in tailoring and crafts to help women earn a stable income.
School Kit Distribution
Distributing school bags and stationery at the beginning of every academic year to children in need.
Nutrition Support Program
Monthly dry‑ration support for vulnerable families identified by our field volunteers.
Youth Leadership Clubs
Engaging local youth in life‑skills, leadership and community service activities.
