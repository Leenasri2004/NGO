Volunteers

Join Our Volunteer Family

Volunteers are the heart of our work at Nathi Foundation. Your time, skills and passion help us run education programs, health camps and community initiatives in and around Tiruppathur.

Whether you are a student, working professional or retired, we have flexible opportunities for you to contribute on weekdays or weekends.

  • Support children’s education and after‑school programs
  • Help in health awareness and community events
  • Assist with fundraising and campaigns
  • Use your professional skills (IT, design, accounts, etc.)

Fill the simple form to register your interest. Our team will contact you within 3–5 working days.
